The Joy of 9:
		1. Why multiples of 9 have digit sums that are multiples of 9:

		9 × 4 = 36 → 3 + 6 = 9
		9 × 17 = 153 → 1 + 5 + 3 = 9
		9 × 123 = 1107 → 1 + 1 + 0 + 7 = 9, This works becuase in base-10, 10≡1(mod 9), so a number is congruent to the sum of its digits modulo 9.
							This leads to the classic arithmetic-checking technique called casting out nines. Lets think a magic
							trick, lets start with a number 1-10, next, triple it, now add 6, next triple it again, next take your
							answer and add the digits. Albeit your number is 9. 

							This works because lets call our number x, triple x so now 3x then add 6 so 3x+6, then triple again,
							so we have 3(3x+6), a multiple of nine.

							Ex:
							let num = 9;
							console.log(num % 9);<-----this is the simplest form of finding multiples of 9
